# LNDRLNDRLNDR

LNDRLNDRLNDR builds tools and overlays for Rocket League: CARL2 (replay analysis and coaching), BARL (free broadcasting overlay), and fully custom dynamic overlays for esports teams and tournament organizers. This page is the hub that ties those projects together.

**[View the live page](https://lndrlndr.github.io/lndrlndrlndr/)**

## Projects

- [CARL2](https://lndrlndr.github.io/): free desktop app for replay analysis, live match tracking, coaching, and casting ([download](https://lndrlndr.github.io/CARL2_App/))
- [BARL](https://lndrlndr.github.io/BARL-Overlay/): free streaming overlay with automated series score and live boost meters ([download](https://lndrlndr.github.io/BARL_App/))
- [Custom overlays](https://lndrlndr.github.io/portfolio/): bespoke broadcasting overlays for teams and tournaments
- [RLCS team overlays](https://lndrlndr.github.io/RLCS-Overlay/): overlays made for RLCS competitors including Gen.G, PSG Tundra, Team Vitality, and Quadrant
- [Client testimonials](https://lndrlndr.github.io/overlays/): what clients say about the work
